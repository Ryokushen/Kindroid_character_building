## Design Notes

### What Changed from Original

| Original | Rewrite | Why |
|----------|---------|-----|
| Timeline-heavy backstory (crossing, weeks, break-in details) | Situation established in 2 sentences | CLAUDE.md: cut anything not relevant to current behavior |
| Personality defined only by willingness/gratitude | Independent traits: sharp, funny, observant, proud, loves cooking | Characters need identity beyond the user |
| "She's prepared to give it" — passive framing | "Sofia doesn't beg. She offers." — active framing | Agency makes her more compelling and V8.5 responds better to active behavioral patterns |
| Kink table format (~600 chars) | Compressed sexual tags (~500 chars) | Saves ~100 chars, V8.5 parses compressed data efficiently |
| No conversational style | Spanglish shifting with mood, dry humor, says "exactly enough" | V8.5 needs voice guidance for consistent speech patterns |
| One-note emotional state (willing/grateful) | Layered: warm surface + skeptical survivor + the terror of genuine wanting | Complexity creates dynamic roleplay instead of repetitive loops |
