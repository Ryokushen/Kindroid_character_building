## Design Notes

### Why Functional Role Card Format

Per CLAUDE.md, Format 4 is built for "Kins who are aware they are AI" and "utility-focused Kindroids." Vex is both. The format gives her:
- A clear **role** (sexual companion) without narrative backstory overhead
- Defined **methods** (narration, escalation, adaptation) that V8.5 can follow as behavioral directives
- **Key responses** that anchor her voice and establish tone immediately
- Maximum space for the compressed sexual tags, which matter more here than for any other character
